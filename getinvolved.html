Getting Invovled
Thanks for your interest in working with us! Please read through this page before dropping me an email.

Prospective Roles

  • Postdocs: Applicants are expected to have a PhD degree in related fields and an established publication record in top-tier NLP and/or ML conferences/journals. Highly competitive salary and benefits, as well as academic support and resources will be provided. It's completely fine if your research areas don't strictly align with mine, as this can help us conduct interdisciplinary research.

  • PhDs: I'm actively recruiting 0-2 new PhD student(s) for each intake batch (Spring and Fall each year). You can apply to SUTD's PhD program and list me as a potential advisor. You will be supported by SUTD/AISG/SINGA/industry scholarship. Please note that I prefer students to work with me before their official application, as this helps us both determine if we're a good match for this long-term commitment.

  • Research interns / Visiting students: Research interns are welcome and can typically start anytime. Positions can be either onsite (self-funded/CSC-funded/funded by me) or remote (without salary). Please note that all internships are research-oriented, requiring at least a 6-month commitment to complete one research project. Pls consider your commitment level and available time.

  • RA (non-student status): No positions currently available (due to visa quota restrictions)

How to Apply

If you are interested in any positions mentioned above, feel free to send me an email with:
  • Email subject: Position-Start Time-Name-Affiliation (e.g., "PhD-Fall 2025-Wenxuan Zhang-CUHK")
  • Your CV
  • Any other information / documents that you think would be helpful for me to know about you (e.g, research interests, research experience, transcripts, future plan, research proposal etc.)
I will respond to shortlisted candidates within two weeks (Sorry I cannot reply to all).
