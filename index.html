About me [Short bio]

I am currently a tenure-track Assistant Professor at the Information Systems Technology and Design (ISTD) Pillar, Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), under the prestigious "SUTD Assistant Professorship (SAP)" scheme (a competitive research award for early-career faculty). Before this, I was a research scientist at Alibaba Group Singapore. I received my Ph.D. from The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

My research interests lie broadly in natural language processing and machine learning, though most of my research are centered around large language models these days. I am passinate to advance NLP models that are inclusive, supporting diverse languages and cultures (e.g., multilingual large language models), while also trustworthy through techniques that improve understanding, safety and robustness of language models.

Current and Planned Research Projects (more on research page):
  • [Multilingual] Understanding and enhancing multilingual capabilities of LLMs (SeaLLMs, Babel)
  • [Accessiblity] Making LLMs more accessible, through model compression or model collaboration
  • [Safety] Ensuring safety of LLMs from development to deployment (MultiJail, Safety Neuron)
  • [Evaluation] Comprehensive and fair evaluation of foundation models (M3Exam, Auto-Arena)
  • [Mechanism] Understaning what is happening inside LLMs (Knowledge & Reasoning)
